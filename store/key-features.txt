
Key features at a glance:

* Chat with anyone out of billions of people -- using their e-mail address!
* Instant messaging and synchronization across devices
* Automatic end-to-end encryption using the Autocrypt Level 1 standard
* Downloads for Android, iOS, Mac OS, Linux with Windows in the works.
* Open Source development with many contributors and a diverse community.
* No Spam -- only messages of known or accepted users are displayed by default.
* Standards based and collaborations ongoing with other decentralization projects.
